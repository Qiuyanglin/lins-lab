Qiuyang Lin joined the **School of Microelectronics at Fudan University** in 2026 as an **Assistant Professor and Ph.D. Supervisor**, and established the **Biomedical Integrated Circuits and Systems Laboratory**.

<!--more-->

![Fudan University](fudan.jpg)

The research of the lab focuses on:

- Biomedical sensing integrated circuits  
- Analog and mixed-signal circuit design  
- Data converters  
- Emerging sensor interface circuits  

The group aims to develop **next-generation biomedical sensing chips and intelligent healthcare systems**.
